Package Overview

This is a perfect trek for beginners as well as experienced trekkers who wish to escape city life but have limited time. Our 4-day trek offers a short, sweet, and delightful experience.

Dayara Bugyal is one of the highest meadows in India, accessible year-round. Depending on the season, you can enjoy lush greenery or snow-laden trails. It's a trek suitable for all age groups, often called a 'family trek,' ideal for children, siblings, parents, and even grandparents.

Whether you want to experience the freshness of spring, the warm sun in summer, the golden hues of autumn, or the snowy wonderland of winter, Dayara Bugyal has something to offer every season.

Each visit feels like stepping into a magnificent canvas. You can capture breathtaking photos, enjoy a picnic, sleep under the starry sky, and perhaps even witness a rare cosmic event.

Beyond the mountains, Dayara Bugyal is rich in flora and fauna, making it a haven for nature enthusiasts. The forests feature a diverse range of trees, including oak, maple, cedar, and rhododendrons. During summer, the meadow blooms with vibrant alpine flowers like Himalayan blue poppies, daisies, and primroses. If you're lucky, you might spot the state bird of Uttarakhand, the Himalayan Monal, among other wildlife.

The trek begins from Natin village to the Gui campsite, where we stay overnight before ascending to Dayara Bugyal the next day.

From the summit, you'll witness the majestic Greater Himalayan range, including peaks like Bandarpunch, Kala Nag, Srikanth, Jaonli, and Draupadi Ka Danda. The sunset over these towering summits is a breathtaking sight that trekkers cherish.

After exploring Dayara Bugyal, we return to the Gui campsite and descend back to Natin village, marking the end of this serene and unforgettable journey.

Itinerary

- Pickup from Prince Chowk, near Dehradun Railway Station at 7:00 a.m.
- Embark on a 185 km road journey to Raithal via Chinyalisaur and Uttarkashi.
- Enjoy breakfast and lunch during travel (self-paid).
- Arrival at Natin village and check into the homestay.
- Rest, followed by dinner and overnight stay.

- After breakfast, start your trek to Gui Campsite (5 km trek through forest patches).
- Arrival at Gui Campsite, followed by a hot lunch.
- Evening acclimatization walk, refreshments, and snacks.
- Dinner and overnight stay at the Gui Campsite.

- Post breakfast, ascend through Oak and Rhododendron forests to Dayara Bugyal.
- Reach Dayara Viewpoint and enjoy packed lunch with scenic views.
- Return to Gui Campsite for evening refreshments and snacks.
- Dinner and overnight stay at the Gui Campsite.

- After breakfast, descend to Natin village.
- Begin the 185 km drive back to Dehradun, with expected arrival by 9:30 PM.
