Package Overview

Commence from Haridwar, culminating in Badrinath amidst the majestic Himalayan hills and breathtaking vistas.

Experience divine ambiance, serene treks, and spiritual moments throughout this spiritual odyssey.

Detailed Itinerary

Begin your journey from Haridwar; our representative will pick you up and transfer you to Barkot in a private car. En route, marvel at the charming beauty of Mussoorie, including a visit to Kempty Falls for water activities. Upon arrival in Barkot, check into the hotel, enjoy a delicious dinner, and retire for the night.

Wake up early and admire the magnificent Bandarpoonch peak and stunning landscapes. After breakfast, proceed towards Yamunotri Dham. Embark on a 6 KM trek from Janki Chatti to Yamunotri Dham. Take a refreshing water bath in Jamnabai Kund before the trek. Visit Yamunotri Temple for Darshan and Pooja, and then head to Surya Kund. Trek back to Jan Ki Chatti and return to Barkot for dinner and overnight stay.

Wake up amidst the spiritual ambiance of Barkot and have breakfast. Proceed from Barkot to Uttarkashi, known as Devbhumi, situated along the Bhagirathi river. Explore Uttarkashi's natural beauty and visit Vishwanath Temple and Shakti Temple. Return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Wake up early to enjoy majestic views of the Deodar trees and mountains. After breakfast, head towards Gangotri via Gangnani, where you can visit Garam Kund. Continue your journey to Gangotri, enjoying scenic views of Harshil valley. Take a blessed dip in the sacred river Ganges at Shree Gangotri and worship Goddess Ganga. Return to Uttarkashi for dinner and overnight stay.

Wake up early and proceed to Guptkashi after breakfast. Enjoy beautiful views of the Ganga and Bhagirathi rivers on the way. Stopover at Srinagar for lunch and continue the journey, witnessing mesmerizing views of Rudraprayag and Ukhimath. Upon reaching Guptkashi, check into the hotel and visit Ardhnarishwar Temple if time permits. Enjoy dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Wake up to stunning mountain views and have breakfast at the hotel. Proceed towards Gaurikund and then to the majestic Kedarnath peak. Begin the 14 km trek from Gauri Kund to Kedarnath Temple or opt for ponies or palanquin rides. Visit the Kedarnath Temple, join the evening aarti, and soak in the spiritual ambiance. Stay overnight at the hotel in Kedarnath.

Participate in the morning Pooja at one of the 12 jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva. Enjoy the captivating beauty of the temple against the backdrop of the grand Kedarnath range. Return to Gaurikund, and then to Guptkashi, where you can relax and have dinner. Retire for the night and recharge for the next day.

Proceed towards Badrinath after breakfast. Upon arrival, explore the scenic beauty of the Neelkanth mountain peak and the confluence of the Alaknanda and Mandakini rivers. Check into the hotel, enjoy dinner, and spend a peaceful night.

Visit the famous Badrinath Temple after breakfast. Take a dip in the sacred Tapt Kund before visiting the temple. Explore the beauty of the temple amidst the Nar and Narayan mountain ranges. Return to the hotel for lunch, then depart for Haridwar. Reach Haridwar and bid farewell to the beautiful journey, carrying unforgettable memories.
