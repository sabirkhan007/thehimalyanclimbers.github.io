Package Overview

Kedarkantha is one of the most demanding mountain peak of the Himalayan region which is situated in Uttarakhand, India. The peak elevates about 12,500 ft in height. This most promising peak is situated within the Govind Wildlife Sanctuary in the Uttarkashi district of Uttarakhand. A trek to the peak of Kedarkantha begins from Sankri; a base camp. Sankri is a small village and trekking generally takes 5 days and 4 nights to complete.

At Trip The Himalayan Climbers for Kedarkantha Lets Do Some Toofani and experience the thrill of Trekking which would be the bundle of Stunning and Awestruck sensation which not only oozes the goose bumps but also leave a satisfactory mark of being witness for such a remarkable trailing.

No doubt the summit is really cherishing since its beginning till the winding up. It begins with beautiful clearings which leads to mindboggling campsites via enormous vegetation at the hilly region. Being a quality linkers for trekkers’ WE at Trip My Soul do empathies with their expectations.

Itinerary

🚗 Distance: 235 km | ⏳ Duration: 9-10 hours
📍 Altitude: 1,878 m
- Early morning pickup from Dehradun Railway Station.
- Scenic drive through Mori, Purola, and Sankri.
- Arrive at Khanyasni Camp, equipment check, and briefing.
- Dinner and overnight stay at campsite.

🚶‍♂ Trek Distance: 5 km | ⏳ Duration: 3-4 hours
📍 Altitude: 2,000 m
- Trek through oak and rhododendron forests.
- Reach Sarutal Camp, a serene high-altitude meadow.
- Leisure time, exploration, and dinner.
- Overnight stay at campsite.

🚶‍♂ Trek Distance: 8 km | ⏳ Duration: 5-6 hours
📍 Altitude: 2,600 m
- Gradual ascent through meadows and forests.
- Arrive at Badang Camp, famous for sunrise & sunset views.
- Evening storytelling sessions and acclimatization.
- Overnight stay at campsite.

🚶‍♂ Trek Distance: 10 km | ⏳ Duration: 6-7 hours
📍 Altitude: 3,800 m (Vijay Top)
- Early morning trek to Vijay Top Summit.
- Breathtaking 360° Himalayan views of Swargarohini, Bandarpoonch, and Black Peak.
- Return trek to Badang Camp.
- Overnight stay at campsite.

🚶‍♂ Trek Distance: 7 km | ⏳ Duration: 4-5 hours
🚗 Drive Distance: 33 km
- Descend through forests and streams to Bhitri Village.
- Drive to Sankri, stay in a guesthouse.
- Explore Sankri village and its wooden architecture.
- Overnight stay at guesthouse.

🚗 Drive Distance: 210 km | ⏳ Duration: 9 hours
- Breakfast and farewell at Sankri.
- Scenic drive back to Dehradun.
- Drop-off at Dehradun Railway Station by 6:00 PM.
