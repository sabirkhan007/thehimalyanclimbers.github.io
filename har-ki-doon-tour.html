Detailed Itinerary

Start your journey from Haridwar early morning. Drive through picturesque landscapes via Mussoorie and Purola to reach Sankri village. This charming village serves as the base camp for the Har Ki Doon trek.
Overnight Stay: Guesthouse at Sankri.

After breakfast, start your trek from Sankri to Pauni Garaat. The trail passes through scenic pine forests, crystal-clear streams, and local villages like Taluka.
Overnight Stay: Tented accommodation at Pauni Garaat.

Trek through beautiful meadows and oak forests while enjoying views of snow-capped peaks. Pass through the picturesque Osla village, known for its ancient architecture and cultural heritage.
Overnight Stay: Tents at Kalkattiyadhaar.

Today is the highlight of the trek! Walk through alpine meadows to reach the majestic Har Ki Doon Valley. Enjoy panoramic views of Swargarohini and other Himalayan peaks. Spend time exploring the valley before trekking back to Kalkattiyadhaar.
Overnight Stay: Tents at Kalkattiyadhaar.

Begin your descent from Kalkattiyadhaar to Pauni Garaat. Enjoy the serene forests and occasional bird sightings as you make your way back.
Overnight Stay: Tents at Pauni Garaat.

Trek back to Sankri, retracing your journey through the forest trails. Celebrate the completion of your trek with a warm dinner in Sankri.
Overnight Stay: Guesthouse at Sankri.

After breakfast, drive back to Haridwar with memories of a lifetime. En route, enjoy the scenic beauty of the Himalayas.
Arrival: Reach Haridwar by evening. Trek ends with divine blessings!
